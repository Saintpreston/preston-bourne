Warped World

Overview

Final Project for my Core Lab Environments class, Sophomore year, the prompt was to create a virtual space with at least three minutes of exploration time. The project must be done using the Unity Game Engine and only limited amount of third party assets were allowed. The emphasis is on environmental design, world building and interactivity.


Goal

I usually go for more reserved, industrial designs with tame colors. I decided to break the pattern and create psychedelic journey with the option to be experienced in VR. So far I've been able to make a build compatible with the QUEST 2. The visuals are heavily inspired by Midnight Gospel a visual series by the renowned Pendleton Ward, known primarily for Adventure Time.


Next Steps

Currently,
  • Constructing better hard surface models for the house in the first scene and the UFO interior.
  • Tweaking UVs
  • Reimagining the Grey-boxing for a less linear walk-through.
  • Adding more complex C# to increase player's interactivity and agency over the world

A trained eye may notice a few structural problems with the first house, the interior of the ufo, and clouds not being seamless in the final scene. This is still in the draft stage so trust that a lot of the architecture is a placeholder and will be replaced for the final working demo. Currently working on my C# scripts to increase user agency/interactivity & taking a course in hard surface modeling. With finals being over and having more free time, I may even "remix" the project in Unreal Engine as an excuse to practice C++.
