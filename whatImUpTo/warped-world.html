Warped World

Overview

Final Project for my Sophomore year, the prompt is a virtual music video based on any audio at least three minutes long. The project must be done using Unity Game Engine and only limited online FBX imports were allowed. The emphasis is on environmental design, world building and interactivity


Goal

I usually go for more reserved, industrial designs with tame colors. I decided to break the pattern and create VR psychedilic journey. The song of choice is Rejjie Snow - Acid Trip and the visuals are heavily inspired by Midnight Gospel a visual series by the world renowned Pendleton Ward, known primarily for Adventure Time.


Next Steps

Currently,

  • Modeling more assets for the scene in Blender
  • Building city, valley & underwater landscapes
  • Scripting in C# to increase player's interactivity and agency over the world
  • Synchronizing the pacing and player interactions with audio
